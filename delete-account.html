Delete Your StackAI Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the StackAI app:

  1. Open StackAI
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion

This will permanently delete your account and all associated data including your stack, lab results, cycles, and chat history.
