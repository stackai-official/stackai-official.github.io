StackAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

StackAI is operated by Tyas Heikkinen, an individual based in Idaho, United States. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the StackAI mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly including your name, email address, password, compounds in your stack, lab results, cycle logs, and chat messages. We also collect usage data automatically including device type, app version, and interaction patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the StackAI service, process your subscription payments, send you dose reminder notifications you have opted into, and provide AI-powered analysis of your health data.

4. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services that may receive your data:

  • Supabase — stores your account and health data securely
  • Anthropic — processes your chat messages and lab results for AI analysis
  • RevenueCat — processes subscription purchases and status
  • Google AdMob — shows ads to free tier users, may collect device identifiers
  • Railway — hosts our backend infrastructure

5. Health Data

StackAI allows you to log health-related information including lab results and supplement protocols. This information is stored securely and used only to provide you with the app features. We do not sell your health data to any third party. All AI analysis is performed to help you understand your own data.

6. Data Security

We implement industry standard security measures including encrypted data transmission, secure authentication, and row-level security ensuring users can only access their own data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the Settings screen in the app.

8. Children's Privacy

StackAI is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover we have collected data from a minor we will delete it immediately.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at support@stackai.app to exercise these rights.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions contact: support@stackai.app
